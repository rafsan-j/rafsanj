Project 4: Integration of Enhanced Direct Contact Membrane Distillation and Parabolic Trough Reflector: A Sustainable Solution for the Water Quality Issues in Bangladesh

This project addresses Bangladesh's water quality challenges by integrating enhanced Direct Contact Membrane Distillation (DCMD) with Parabolic Trough Reflectors (PTR). I contributed to the research and development of a self-sufficient, reliable, and sustainable water purification system. This involved modifying DCMD with superhydrophobic coatings and vortex generators, and utilizing PTR with metallic wastes for heating. The project also included a self-sufficient system using Photovoltaic (PV) cells. This project provides a daily average of 5 liters of safe distilled water per person within the water plant range.

Key Contributions: Research, system design, water purification, renewable energy integration.

Key Skills: Water Purification, Renewable Energy, Engineering Design, Research, Problem Solving.
